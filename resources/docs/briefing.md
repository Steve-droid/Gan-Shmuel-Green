# Gan Shmuel Project - Briefing

Gan Shmuel is a kibbuts in Israel with a large juice factory. Their old weighing system has broken down, and they need a complete digital transformation.

Your mission: Build a modern system that tracks fruit deliveries, weighs incoming trucks, and calculates accurate payments for producers.
This is a real-world microservices project where three teams must work together to deliver a complete solution.
